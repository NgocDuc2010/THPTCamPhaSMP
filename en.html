⌨ Commands

Server Commands

  • Open Main Menu: /menu
  • Fast Travel to Public Zones: /warp <name> (Ex: top, gacha, thuyenbay, whitehouse, hotel, mecung, event, nether, theend)
  • Teleport to Player: /tpa <player name> (Cancel: /tpcancel, Accept: /tpaccept, Deny: /tpdeny)
  • Wild Teleport (Survival): Teleport to a random wilderness area to build a base: /rpm
  • Server Store: Open basic items shop: /shop
  • Missions: Open daily/weekly quest board to earn money: /quest
  • Transfer Money: Pay funds to another player: /pay <amount> <name>
  • Gambling: Create a Coinflip match: /cf <amount> (View list: /cf)
  • Lottery: Open Server Lottery UI: /lottery gui
  • Black Market (Auction): Open Auction House: /ah (Sell held item: /ah sell <price>)
  • Personal System: View Ranks (/rank), View Titles (/capbac)
  • Housing: Set base location (/sethome), Teleport to base (/home)
  • Support: Send an emergency message to online Admins for bugs/help: /helpop <message>

⚖️ Server Rules

✅ ARTICLE 1: COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

  • Respect each other and communicate politely.
  • Protect public server structures.
  • Trade fairly and transparently.
  • Only use other players' chests/farms with their permission.

❌ ARTICLE 2: PROHIBITED ACTIONS

  • No Hacking/Cheating (X-Ray, Fly, AutoClickers).
  • No griefing structures or stealing from Bases.
  • No bug exploits or item duplication (Dupe).
  • No scamming, toxicity, or chat spam.
  • No Support Abuse: Do not misuse the Suggestion/Report feature to joke, insult, spam, or send false information to Admins.

⚠️ ARTICLE 3: 5 PUNISHMENT TIERS

  • 1. LIGHT: Warning, or Chat Mute (1h - 24h).
  • 2. MEDIUM - LIGHT: Item confiscation, Fines, or Asset seizure.
  • 3. MEDIUM: x2 Damage Compensation, Server Kick, or Rank Demotion.
  • 4. MEDIUM - SEVERE: Jail time (1 to 7 days), full asset freeze.
  • 5. SEVERE: Permanent Account Ban or IP Ban for serious hacking/griefing.
