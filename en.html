Command Center

Commands

Quick access to teleportation, economy, system, and support shortcuts.

1. Teleportation & Travel

  • /dichchuyen smp - Shift through the survival world (⚠️ Warning: Your inventory will be cleared when died!).
  • /rtp - Randomly teleport to start your survival journey and build a base (Only use after entering /dichchuyen smp).
  • /dichchuyen world - Teleport to the safe zone (Old Top area: no inventory loss, no PvP) for sightseeing and trading.
  • /warp <location> - Fast travel to specific server locations.

    Available locations:

    • gacha: Test your luck with exciting Gacha crates.
    • thuyenbay: The flying boat, a scenic viewpoint.
    • whitehouse: The White House, where top players claim rewards.
    • hotel: The underwater resort and entertainment complex.
    • mecung: The giant castle maze.
    • event: The limited-time item trading event area.
    • lab: The Laboratory.
    • nether: The Nether roof.
    • theend: The End dimension.
  • /sethome - Set your current location as your home.
  • /home - Teleport instantly back to your saved home location.
  • /tpa <player name> - Send a teleport request to another player.
  • /tpaccept - Accept an incoming teleport request.
  • /tpdeny - Decline an incoming teleport request.
  • /tpcancel - Cancel a teleport request you recently sent.

2. Economy & Trading

  • /shop - Open the server shop to buy or sell basic items.
  • /quest - View and accept daily tasks to earn money.
  • /pay <amount> <player name> - Send in-game money to another player.
  • /ah - Open the Black Market (Auction House) to view items sold by other players.
  • /ah sell <price> - List the item you are currently holding on the Black Market.

3. Team Commands

  • /team create - Create a new Team.
  • /team deposit <amount> - Deposit money into the Team.
  • /team withdraw <amount> - Withdraw money from the Team (Only the Team owner can withdraw).
  • /team chat - Chat privately within the Team.

4. System & Personal Info

  • /menu - Open the main server menu.
  • /rank - View your personal Rank information.
  • /capbac - View and equip available Titles.
  • /helpop <message> - Send a direct message to online Staff for help or to report an issue.

Server Policy

Server Rules

Guidelines to keep the server fair, safe, and friendly for everyone.

✅ ARTICLE 1: COMMUNITY STANDARDS

  • Respect each other and communicate politely.
  • Preserve and protect the server's public structures.
  • Trade fairly and transparently.
  • Only use farms or chests with the owner's explicit permission.

❌ ARTICLE 2: STRICTLY PROHIBITED ACTIONS

  • Hacking or cheating (e.g., X-Ray, Fly, AutoClicker) is strictly prohibited.
  • Griefing public server structures is prohibited. However, player bases in the survival world CAN be raided or looted. Build your base carefully!
  • Bug abuse and item duplication (duping) are strictly prohibited.
  • Scamming, toxic behavior, and chat spamming are not allowed.
  • Do not misuse the Support/Report feature to troll, insult, spam, or provide false information to the Staff.

⚠️ ARTICLE 3: 5 PUNISHMENT TIERS

  • 1. MINOR: Warning or Chat Mute (1h - 24h).
  • 2. MINOR - MODERATE: Item confiscation, in-game fines, or asset seizure.
  • 3. MODERATE: Double damage compensation, Rank Demotion, etc.
  • 4. MODERATE - SEVERE: Jail time (1 to 7 days) and a complete asset freeze.
  • 5. SEVERE: Permanent Account Ban or IP Ban for severe hacking or griefing.
